"Parages" is a site for experimental narration centered on the texts of Derrida, Blanchot, and private experience.

Website link: https://caravaggiosbacchus.neocities.org/

Parages is a reading experiment in book narrative in the age of hypertext links.Through coded design, visual illustration and
textual editing, Derrida's deconstructive paths are recovered in the new medium, returning to the temporal and spatial experiences of emptiness, disappearance, and dislocation in Blanchot's texts.
